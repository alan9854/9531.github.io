Oolong Tea

Taiwanese Oolong tea consists of Camellia tree leaves and experiences a partial oxidation process which gives it a distinct color and taste.

The best oolong tea neither has characteristics of green nor black teas. It’s a unique tea tracing its origin to the Taiwanese high mountains. The appearance, shape, and flavor of an oolong tea can vary wildly depending on the region where it’s grown and how it’s processed.
